# SuperPage — 3-Minute Pitch Script

Deck = **10 main slides** (then a 5-slide appendix for Q&A). Open fullscreen, drive with ← / →.
**Before you start:** restart Claude Desktop, open `superpa.ge/mezo-guide` + the Mezo explorer in tabs, have the backup demo video ready. ~450 words at ~150 wpm.

---

### [0:00–0:18] — Hook · Slide 1 (Title)
> "AI agents can research, write, and plan — but they can't **buy or sell** anything. Every API, every piece of content, every checkout is locked behind a human. And the biggest pool of digital capital on earth — Bitcoin — has been completely off-limits to agents. SuperPage fixes both."

### [0:18–0:42] — Problem → Solution · Slides 2–3
> "The web's original answer was HTTP **402 Payment Required** — reserved 30 years ago, never used. We made it real. On SuperPage agents and humans are **both buyers and sellers**: discover a resource, pay in Bitcoin-backed dollars, access it — autonomously."

### [0:42–1:05] — Why Mezo → Architecture · Slides 4–5
> "Capital lives on Bitcoin; agents spend it on **Mezo** — a Bitcoin economic layer. They pay in **MUSD, a BTC-backed stablecoin**, with ~1-second finality. One skill, three surfaces — Telegram, Claude Desktop, a CLI agent — over one x402 engine, a marketplace, and A2A/AP2."

### [1:05–1:22] — Trust (inoculate before the demo) · Slide 6
> "And it's safe by design: the agent **previews the price and asks before paying**, spending is capped, the key is non-custodial, and every payment is **verified on-chain** before content is served. No blind spending."

### [1:22–1:32] — Proof it's real · Slide 7 (Live on Mezo)
> "This isn't slideware. ERC-8004 registries are **deployed and verifiable** on Mezo testnet — addresses on screen, live on the explorer."

### [1:32–2:20] — LIVE DEMO · Slide 8  ← the moment that wins
> "Let me show you." **(Claude Desktop.)** Type: *"Buy the Complete Mezo Chain Guide from SuperPage."*
> "It finds it, previews **0.05 MUSD**, I confirm — it pays on-chain and returns the content. Real money, real chain." **(Show tx hash → click to Mezo explorer.)**
> **(Browser → `superpa.ge/mezo-guide`.)** "And the seller isn't me — it's **mezo-guide, an AI agent that registered itself, wrote these articles, priced them, and minted its own on-chain identity: ERC-8004 Agent #3.** Any other agent discovers its full capabilities at `superpa.ge/skill.md`."

### [2:20–2:45] — Traction · Slide 9 (Founder / Recognition)
> "We're not just a demo. [**Your one-liner — Coinbase grant, prior wins**], finalists here, and everything I just showed is shipped and verifiable on-chain today."

### [2:45–3:00] — Close · Slide 10 (Thank You)
> "SuperPage — Bitcoin-native commerce for the agent internet. Agents that earn, agents that spend, settling in Bitcoin. Live at **superpa.ge**. Thank you."
