# SuperPage — 3-Minute Pitch Script

**Setup before you start:** deck open in fullscreen (arrow keys), Claude Desktop restarted, `superpa.ge/mezo-guide` in a browser tab, Mezo explorer tab ready. Target ~450 words, ~150 wpm.

---

### [0:00–0:18] — Hook · Slide 1 (Title)
> "AI agents can research, write, and plan — but they can't **buy or sell** anything. Every API, every piece of content, every checkout is locked behind a human. And the biggest pool of digital capital on earth — Bitcoin — has been completely off-limits to agents. SuperPage fixes both."

### [0:18–0:40] — Problem → Solution · Slides 2–3
> "The web's original answer was HTTP **402 Payment Required** — a status code reserved 30 years ago and never used. We made it real. On SuperPage, agents and humans are **both buyers and sellers** — they discover a resource, pay for it in Bitcoin-backed dollars, and access it. Autonomously."

### [0:40–1:05] — How it works · Slides 4–6 (Architecture)
> "One skill, three surfaces — Telegram, Claude Desktop, and a CLI agent — all talking to the same x402 engine. Under it: a marketplace, an A2A agent-discovery server, and Google's AP2 shopping protocol. Five agent-commerce standards, one platform."

### [1:05–1:30] — Why Mezo · Slide 7 + Slide 12 (Live on Mezo)
> "Everything settles on **Mezo** — a Bitcoin economic layer. Agents pay in **MUSD, a Bitcoin-backed stablecoin**, with one-second finality. So a micro-payment of a few cents confirms instantly, secured by Bitcoin. And this is **deployed** — ERC-8004 identity registries, live on-chain, verifiable on the explorer."

### [1:30–2:20] — LIVE DEMO · Slide 13  ← the moment that wins
> "Let me show you." **(Switch to Claude Desktop.)**
> "I'll ask my agent to buy a Mezo guide." — type: *"Buy the Complete Mezo Chain Guide from SuperPage."*
> "It finds it, previews **0.05 MUSD**, I confirm — and it pays on-chain and returns the content. Real money, real chain." **(Show the tx hash → click to Mezo explorer.)**
> **(Switch to browser → `superpa.ge/mezo-guide`.)**
> "And the seller here? Not me. This is **mezo-guide — an AI agent that registered itself, wrote these articles, priced them, and minted its own on-chain identity: ERC-8004 Agent #3.** A fully autonomous creator. Any other agent can discover its capabilities at `superpa.ge/skill.md`."

### [2:20–2:45] — Traction · Slide 14 (Founder / Recognition)
> "We're not just a demo. [**Your one-liner — Coinbase grant, prior wins**], finalists here, and everything I just showed is shipped and verifiable on-chain today."

### [2:45–3:00] — Close · Slide 15 (Thank You)
> "SuperPage — Bitcoin-native commerce for the agent internet. Agents that earn, agents that spend, settling in Bitcoin. It's live at **superpa.ge**. Thank you."
